The Ethics of Big Data

New York, New York Columbia University

May 17-19, 2023

Dr. danah boyd

Principal Researcher at Microsoft Research and Founder of Data & Society

Brief:

This conference examines the ethical challenges and opportunities presented by the increasing availability and use of big data in fields such as business, government, and academia. Topics include privacy, surveillance, bias, and transparency.

Join us for an engaging and thought-provoking discussion on the ethical dimensions of big data, featuring some of the most prominent voices in the field!
